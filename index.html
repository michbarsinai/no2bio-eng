In Short

Israel's ministry of Interior is building a compulsory biometric database, containing fingerprints and facial image of every citizen. Well, except for citizens serving in certain agencies. We - worried Israeli citizens - are trying to stop this.

What's Here?

Some background materials explaining how the database came to be, news and updates about the database and our efforts to stop it, and some contact information.

Why English?

Outreach. There are many groups around the world dealing with such government initiatives, and most of them don't read Hebrew. The site designed for Israeli citizens is here. We are happy to collaborate with any group facing similar problems.
