About No2Bio

no2bio is an organization of Israeli citizens, working to stop the creation of a compulsory biometric database in Israel. no2bio calls the government to cancel the project, and issue smart id cards with no central database - similar to other western countries.

no2bio is a voluntary organization. None of us are paid for the activities we do.
